ABOUT VITAFIT Health Coaching & Personal training

VitaFit is operated by Mayra Hall, a certified Personal Trainer through the National Academy of Sports Medicine and Health Coach throug the American Council on Exercise. Mayra has practiced and trained various sports including Muay Thai, spinning, long distance running, and handball. She also holds a bachelor's degree in Public Relations from the University of Taubate in Sao Paulo, Brazil.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide quality service to our clients, because we believe in the long term benefits of a healthy lifestyle. We value you as individual, so we create specific workout plans for your needs. Your body is your temple, and that is how we treat it. Vitafit provides Personal training services for those who want to make a change in their lives. We create specific workout plans for all levels of fitness and work in a progressive fashion, so you are constantly improving your fitness level. Because everybody’s body is different, we will make sure to deliver the best workout plan to your needs, always working accordingly to your goals and pushing you towards your best. More than just making you look good, we want to make you feel good!

Specializations

  • WEIGHT LOSS

  • WHAT'S INCLUDED
  • We can help you lose those extra pounds, by developing a specific circuit training with little rest in between exercises that will make your body burn more calories and help to improve your metabolism.

  • STRENGTH TRAINING

  • WHAT'S INCLUDED
  • Do you want to increase your lean muscle mass and get toned? We can help you that, by creating your personalized strength training, based on your needs and goals.

  • FUNCTIONAL TRAINING

  • WHAT'S INCLUDED
  • Wether you want to lose weight or build strength, your body needs to be able to perform the daily activities in an efficient way. We create functional training programs that will lead to better joint mobility and stability, as well as more efficient motor patterns. Improving these factors decreases the potential for an injury sustained during athletic exercises. The benefits may arise from the use of training that emphasizes the body's natural ability to move in six degrees of freedom.

  • WHAT'S INCLUDED
